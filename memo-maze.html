Gameplay

Features

🧠

Memorize-then-navigate

See the maze for a few seconds, then swipe through it blind. The whole game lives in this tight loop.

⚡

Move Sequence Queue

Chain swipes into sequences for 1.5x, 2x, and 3x scoring multipliers — plan ahead, commit fast.

❄

Traps, Ice & Jumps

Push-back traps, slide-chain ice tiles, crumbling floors, and gap-jump mechanics raise the stakes.

💎

Crystal Economy & Glimpse

Earn crystals from stars, spend 50 for a 1.5s Glimpse that reveals the maze again when you're lost.

🏗

30 Hand-Crafted Levels

Five chapters with a clear difficulty curve — First Steps, Twists & Turns, Against the Clock, and more.

🛠

In-Game Level Designer

Tap-to-cycle editor with BFS validation builds a My Levels chapter from your own mazes.
